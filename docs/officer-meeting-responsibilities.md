## Roles Summary

- **President**: Overall club leadership and roadmap
- **VPE**: Education-focused (agenda, roles, speeches, pathways)
- **VPM**: Membership (user management, member contacts, data sync)
- **VPPR**: Public relations (guest contacts)
- **Secretary**: Club records
- **Treasurer**: Finances, guest tracking, reports
- **SAA**: Meeting day operations
